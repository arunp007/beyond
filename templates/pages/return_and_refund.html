RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

Thank you for shopping at Beyond Your Own Comfort Online Nighty Store. We appreciate your business and want to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

1. Return
1.1 Eligibility

You have a days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging, with tags attached.

1.2 Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are non-returnable, including:

  • Personalized or customized items.
  • One Time used items are non-returnable.
  • Damaged items are non-returnable.
2. Initiate a Return
2.1 Contact Us

To initiate a return, please contact us at beyondyourown007@gmail.com or +918547639106 with your order number and details of the item(s) you wish to return. We will provide you with instructions on how to return the item(s) and the return shipping address.

2.2 Return Shipping

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

3. Refunds
3.1 Processing Time

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

3.2 Refund Method

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Return and Refund Policy, please contact us at beyondyourown007@gmail.com or +918547639106.

5. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this Return and Refund Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website.

6. Applicability

This Return and Refund Policy applies only to purchases made from Beyond Your Own Comfort Online Nighty Store. For purchases made from third-party retailers, please refer to their respective return and refund policies.
