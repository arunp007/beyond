SHIPPING POLICY

Thank you for shopping at Beyond Your Own Comfort Online Nighty Store. We are committed to delivering your orders promptly and efficiently. Please review our Shipping Policy to understand how we process and deliver your purchases.

1. Order Processing Time
1.1 Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 14 - 20 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

1.2 Order Confirmation

Upon placing your order, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details. Please review this confirmation email carefully to ensure the accuracy of your shipping address and order information.

2. Shipping Methods
2.1 Shipping Providers

We partner with reputable shipping carriers to deliver your orders safely and efficiently. Our primary shipping carriers include DTDC, Ekart, and Indian Postal Parcel.

2.2 Shipping Options

We offer standard shipping and expedited shipping options for most orders. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout.

3. Shipping Rates
3.1 Rate Calculation

Shipping rates are calculated based on the weight of your order, your location, and the selected shipping method. Shipping costs will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

4. Order Tracking
4.1 Tracking Information

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this tracking information to monitor the status and location of your package during transit.

5. Shipping Restrictions
5.1 International Shipping

At this time, we only offer shipping within India. We do not currently offer international shipping.

6. Delivery Issues
6.1 Delivery Delays

While we make every effort to deliver your orders on time, please note that delivery times may vary due to factors beyond our control, such as weather conditions, carrier delays, or other unforeseen circumstances.

6.2 Lost or Damaged Shipments

In the event that your order is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us immediately for assistance. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure that you receive your order as soon as possible.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Shipping Policy, please contact us at beyondyourown007@gmail.com or +918547639106.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this Shipping Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website.
